Video Time Capsule (VTC)
Founder & Designer · 2023 – 2025
View details ↓
A senior-friendly app experience for recording and sharing elders’ stories with younger generations.
Overview

Video Time Capsule focuses on making storytelling feel natural for elders. The design uses clear visuals, large touch targets, and simple flows so users can spend time talking, not navigating menus.

The project deepened my interest in human-centered design and showed how carefully chosen UI patterns can make technology more inclusive.

Tech & skills: product design, user experience, front-end prototyping, storytelling.

Humans or Haptics – Sports Training Research
National High School Journal of Science · 2022 – 2023
View details ↓
Independent research comparing how athletes learn from human coaches versus robotic training systems.
Overview

This project analyzed biomechanical and cognitive aspects of training, asking how feedback, repetition, and perception differ between human and robotic coaches.

The work culminated in a peer-reviewed publication and strengthened my ability to design studies, interpret data, and write clearly about technical topics.

Skills: research design, data analysis, scientific writing, communicating technical ideas.

AI Hand Gesture Recognition
Python · Accessibility · 2022 – 2023
View details ↓
A Python-based prototype that recognizes hand gestures to support people with speech disabilities.
Overview

This prototype explored search-based algorithms and computer vision techniques to map hand positions to commands or phrases, aiming to provide an alternate communication channel.

Building it gave me practical experience with algorithm design and showed how small technical choices impact both accuracy and user experience.

Tech: Python · algorithms · basic AI concepts · experimentation.
