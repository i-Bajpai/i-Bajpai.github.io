Video Time Capsule (VTC)

Founder & Designer · 2023 – 2025 · vtcmemories.net

Video Time Capsule is an app experience designed to help elders record and share their stories in a way that feels welcoming and intuitive. The interface emphasizes large, clear visuals and simple flows so users can focus on storytelling instead of navigating menus.

Working on VTC strengthened my interest in human-centered design and showed me how thoughtful UI choices can make technology more inclusive across generations.

Humans or Haptics: Robots for Sports Training

National High School Journal of Science · 2022 – 2023

For this research project, I explored whether athletes learn more effectively from human coaches or robotic training systems. The work examined both biomechanical and cognitive aspects of training, looking at how feedback, repetition, and perception influence performance.

The project culminated in a peer-reviewed publication and gave me experience designing a study, interpreting data, and communicating results clearly to both technical and non-technical audiences.

AI Hand Gesture Recognition

Python · 2022 – 2023

Built a prototype gesture-recognition system in Python aimed at supporting people with speech disabilities. The system uses a search-based approach to interpret hand positions and map them to commands or phrases.

This project deepened my interest in AI for accessibility and taught me how algorithm choices affect both accuracy and user experience.

TEDx Santa Cruz Talk

Speaker · September 15, 2024

I spoke at TEDx Santa Cruz about the motivation behind Video Time Capsule and why capturing intergenerational stories matters. The talk included audience participation and emphasized the role of empathy and design in building tools that people actually want to use.

Winning Edge Podcast Feature

Guest · November 2023

As a guest on the Winning Edge Podcast, I discussed what it looks like to balance competitive table tennis, rigorous schoolwork, and side projects. We focused on time management, resilience, and how sports and academics reinforce each other.
